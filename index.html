🌿 Rainy Season, Baby

Late March is the start of rainy season in Medellín. Expect sunny mornings and dramatic afternoon showers that pass quickly. We built this itinerary around that rhythm — outdoors early, restaurants and covered spots in the afternoon. Pack a compact umbrella, a light rain jacket, and an adventurous spirit. The lush greens and misty mountains after a shower are breathtaking.

🧳 Where to Drop Your Bags

Check out at noon, fly at 8pm — here's how to stay free all day:

Option 1 — Heiss Hotel Front Desk (Free) ✦ Best Bet

Ask the front desk when you check in on Day 1 to confirm they'll hold bags after checkout. Most El Poblado hotels do this for free. Swing back after the city tour, grab bags, Uber to MDE.

Option 2 — Bounce App (~$3.50/bag) ✦ Backup

Bounce partner at Selina Hostel, 7 min from Parque Lleras in El Poblado. Locked room, bags sealed with zip ties, $10K insurance. Book via bounce.com — pay with card, no cash needed.

Option 3 — LuggageHero (~$1.49/hr) ✦ Pay by the Hour

Only service with hourly rates in Medellín. Verified spots near Poblado Metro. Book at luggagehero.com

⚠️ MDE Airport Has No Luggage Storage

José María Córdova International has no storage. Handle bags in El Poblado before heading out.
